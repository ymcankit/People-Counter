People Counter

Choose the Counting Method

There are two methods for the counting .First one is model detecting with tracking and second is contours.In Model detection and tracking method first detction is made on frame and filtered for persons this detection process is run on every 7 frame and for rest of the time a traceker is placed based on the previous detection and using centroid tracking algorithm centroids is calculated for every detection along with distances between them and path of these centroids is observed to count the person entering and exsiting ,This method is also valid for another classe like car etc.so this can also become car counting process. The contours method is simple if you know that ther is going to be only one type of object then this method is very well suited and fastest , it just detect the differnece in frame (mask) and provide the boxes of moving object and these boxes can be tracked for their direction hence counting process is available
